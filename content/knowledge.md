## Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Do I need experience or a license to drive a speedboat?
A: No experience or license is required. Our certified guides provide full instructions before every tour.

Q: Is there an age or weight restriction?
A: Participants must be at least 16 years old to drive. Weight limit is 250 lbs per person. Children under 16 can ride as passengers.

Q: Do I need to know how to swim?
A: Not required for speedboat tours. Life jackets are provided. Basic comfort in water is recommended for snorkeling.

Q: Are there health restrictions?
A: Guests with heart conditions, back problems, or who are pregnant should consult their doctor before booking.

Q: Is alcohol allowed?
A: No alcohol during the tour. Upon completion, guests receive 2 complimentary shots at the Lime Wedge bar.

Q: How long are the tours?
A: 30-minute express, 2-hour, and full-day options available. Contact us for specifics.

Q: Where do tours depart?
A: Private dock at Malcom Park East Bay Street, Arawak Cay, Nassau, next to Antiki Floating Bar.

Q: How do I book?
A: Call 242-817-2944, email cruisingadventuresltd@gmail.com, or visit our Book an Adventure page.

Q: Do you offer group packages?
A: Yes! Birthday parties, corporate events, bachelor parties and more. Contact us for group pricing.

Q: Do you offer couples packages?
A: Yes, our Love Birds package is designed for couples with a private speedboat experience.
